Assignment (09/02/2026)
Assignment Name: AI Around Me
Description: List 10 real-life AI applications you use daily and explain how ML might work behind each in 2 lines.


1. Smartphone Face Unlock
Uses machine learning models trained on facial features to recognize and match my face with stored data for secure access.

2. Google Search Engine
ML analyzes my search history, location, and popular queries to rank and personalize search results.

3. YouTube/Netflix Recommendations
Recommendation algorithms learn from my watch history and preferences to suggest videos or movies I’m likely to enjoy.

4. Voice Assistants (Siri / Google Assistant / Alexa)
Speech recognition models convert voice to text and NLP models understand intent to generate responses.

5. Email Spam Filter
ML models classify emails by learning patterns from previously marked spam and legitimate messages.

6. Google Maps Navigation
ML predicts traffic conditions by analyzing real-time data from users, past traffic trends, and GPS signals.

7. Social Media Feeds (Instagram / Facebook / X)
Algorithms analyze my interactions (likes, comments, shares) to rank posts that appear on my feed.

8. Autocorrect & Predictive Text
Language models learn from typing patterns and common word sequences to suggest or correct words.

9. Online Shopping Recommendations (Amazon / Flipkart)
ML tracks browsing and purchase history to recommend products similar to my interests.

10. Photo Enhancement & Filters
Computer vision models detect faces, lighting, and objects to automatically enhance images or apply filters.
